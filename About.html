About

 

Josie Kwan, is a Chinese-American poet, writer, and artist. She has performed in multiple coffee shops, school shows, and poetry competetions. Poetry is her favorite way to express herself. She doesn't shy away from the *albeit* difficult and unpleast topics. Josie believes in breaking down the stigma around mental health, stopping asian hate, and fighting againt police brutality. She also believes in herself though the journey there was a long one and she would like to thank everyone that helped her get here and especially the ones that stuck around. Josie's journey is far from over and her desire to reach people with her writing is still growing. Next on her list, get publish.
