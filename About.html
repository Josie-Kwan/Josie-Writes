About

 

Josie Kwan is a Chinese-American poet, writer, and artist. She has performed in coffee shops, school shows, and poetry competitions. Poetry is her favorite way to express herself. She doesn't shy away from the albeit difficult and unpleasant topics. Josie believes in breaking down the stigma around mental health, stopping asian hate, and fighting against police brutality. She also believes in herself throughout the journey there was a long one, and she would like to thank everyone who helped her get here, especially the ones who stuck around. Josie's journey is far from over, and her desire to reach people with her writing is still growing. Next on her list, get published.
